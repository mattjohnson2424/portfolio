Cryptocurrency Mining Rig

An investor approached me with the funds to construct a cryptocurrency mining rig. Once I had the funds to start ordering the various components, my desk quickly filled up with boxes containing parts.

Because of the overpriced rig frames due to the crypto mining bullrun, which was currently taking place, I built my own frame which saved me around $100.

I had to stop constuction with only 3 GPUs installed because some ATX power cables did not fit correctly.

The rig is currently up and running, outputting ~400 Mh/s, generating ~$30 a day, and using ~900 W of power.
