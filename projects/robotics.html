Robotics

In 5th grade, I was one of the lead designers in our school's robotics team. We ended up advancing to the state semi-finals.

I managed a team to design and build a lego EV3 robot to traverse a course and perform various tasks.

We used a specialized arm, attached to the robot, that held a medium sized motor. The arm was a 4-point lift arm (pictured) that moved with great precision and allowed us to perform actions with great dexterity.
