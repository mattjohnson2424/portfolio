Stock Screening Program

Inspired by my oldest brother (a UGA alum and Finance major from the Terry College of Business), we began to work on a project combining our two fields, Finance and Computer Science .

I am currently designing a new program in Python that will send specific alerts when a stock reaches certain buy or sell criteria.

This program will import historical stock market data from various companies and use indicators such as RSI, MACD, Support/Resistance, and Volume Indicators to determine whether the program should set off an alert.
